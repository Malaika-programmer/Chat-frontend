Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with ChatConnect?

Simply visit our website and click "Start Chatting" to join a chat room. No registration required!

Is ChatConnect free to use?

Yes! ChatConnect is completely free for all users. We may introduce premium features in the future.

Are my conversations private?

ChatConnect uses secure WebSocket connections. However, please note that messages in public rooms are visible to all participants.

Can I create my own chat rooms?

Absolutely! Use the "New Room" button in the chat interface to create custom rooms for your discussions.

What devices are supported?

ChatConnect works on any modern web browser on desktop, tablet, or mobile devices.

How can I report inappropriate behavior?

Use the report feature in chat rooms or contact our support team directly.
